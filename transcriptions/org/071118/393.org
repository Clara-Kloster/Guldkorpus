*** Notes
The date "mandagh næst effter dysmøsse" is tentatively interpreted as 11 October (Reporitorium), which would indicate "dysmøsse" is meant to mean the feast of St. Denis (9 Oct). However, a preferable interpretation is "dødsmesse", i.e. 2 November, giving the diploma a date of 8 November.
